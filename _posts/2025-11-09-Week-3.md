### 2. Data Pipeline Logic
1.  [cite_start]**Input:** The `getUserMedia` API captures webcam frames[cite: 17].
2.  [cite_start]**Processing:** TF.js extracts eye landmarks (e.g., coordinates 43-48) in real-time[cite: 17].
3.  [cite_start]**Calculation:** The system calculates the Y-coordinate of the eye's center and normalizes it (0 to 1)[cite: 18, 19].
4.  [cite_start]**Action:** This normalized value is directly mapped to the paddle's vertical position[cite: 20].
