## ðŸ’¥ Error Board (Analysis & Fixes)
We identified several edge cases where the model failed during testing.

### 1. Lighting & Reflection
* **Issue:** Strong light glare on glasses was captured as a feature, causing detection failure.
* **Fix:** Collect additional data with backlighting and strong indoor lighting conditions.

![Lighting Issue](/assets/images/error-lighting.jpg)

### 2. Motion Blur
* **Issue:** Rapid head movement caused blurry images, making eye features indistinguishable.
* **Fix:** Apply 'motion blur' effects during data augmentation and train with intentionally blurry images.

### 3. Occlusion
* **Issue:** Hair or thick eyeglass frames covering the eyes were misclassified as 'Half-Opened'.
* **Fix:** Intensively collect data of partially occluded eyes.

### 4. Distance & Squinting
* **Issue:** Accuracy dropped when the user was too far or squinting.
* **Fix:** Add training data for short distances and clearly visible eye shapes.
